Napa Valley Lodging Rooms & Rates

Enter your room at our Napa Valley lodging at Churchill Manor through beautiful hand painted doors and discover the old-world craftsmanship and elegance of an 1889 mansion. The rooms at our Napa Valley accommodation also feature modern amenities to offer the ultimate in guest comfort. Each room is named after a Napa Valley wine growing regions, called “Appellations”—these have been painted on each door by a local Napa artist.
